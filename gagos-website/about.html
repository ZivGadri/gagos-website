About Gagos Development

Gagos Development is a real estate company based in Cyprus, focused on creating high-quality residential properties in the Larnaca region — with a special emphasis on the picturesque village of Oroklini.

Founded by seasoned developers with a passion for thoughtful design and long-term value, our mission is to develop homes that serve as both comfortable living spaces and smart investments. We believe that real estate is not just about building structures, but about shaping communities and improving lives.

Our team is deeply familiar with the local market, regulations, and construction landscape. We work closely with architects, contractors, and partners to ensure every detail meets the highest standards — from land acquisition to project delivery.

Whether you’re looking for a family home, a holiday property, or a strategic investment, Gagos Development delivers projects you can trust.
